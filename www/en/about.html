About

Mi Flamenco Place is a journey across the flamenco dimension of the city of Sevilla, collective-driven by the people who do and love flamenco. If flamenco was already declared by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage, Sevilla must be considered one of its most important capitals, because of its history and tradition, of course, but also due to the huge amount of cultural, formative and leisure activities it has to offer. Thanks to the help of Ministerio de Cultura, Mi Flamenco Place is born, a new app project created by SURNAMES, Narradores Transmedia that intends to highlight the rich flamenco dimension of Sevilla, past and present of this art, and beyond mere geolocation thanks to the emotional connections between three basic coordinates: places, people and stories.
